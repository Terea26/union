Welcome to Hotel Union

Where Prague's Story Still Breathes

Built in 1910 to the designs of architect Jaroslav Benedikt, Hotel Union has welcomed travellers, writers and dreamers for over a century.

Just minutes from the city centre yet wrapped in the calm of the Nusle valley, our family-run house offers a refined base for discovering Prague.
